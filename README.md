# E-Commerce-Store-Analysis
A data analytics project that explores sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends in an online retail environment. The dashboard highlights key KPIs such as revenue, profit margin, payment preferences, shipping modes, and category performance, helping businesses make smarter, data‑driven decisions

## Key (KPIs) Questions
-What is the total quarterly sales revenue and how does it trend across Q1–Q4?

-What is the overall profit margin compared to total sales?

-Which state contributes the highest sales (e.g., Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh)?

-Which customer contributes the most revenue and how consistent is their buying behavior?

-Which payment mode (COD, UPI, Credit/Debit Card, EMI) is most preferred by customers?

-Which product category (Clothing, Electronics, Furniture) drives the majority of sales?

-Which sub‑categories (Printers, Bookcases, Sarees, Accessories, Tables) generate the highest profit?

-How does monthly profit fluctuate across the year, and which months show peaks or dips?

-What is the average order value (AOV) across states and categories?

-Which cities or regions show the highest order volumes, and how can they be leveraged for growth?

🔍 Project Insights
Strong Sales Performance: Total quarterly sales reached 438K with 37K profit and 5615 quantity sold.

Regional Leaders: Maharashtra (102K) and Madhya Pradesh (87K) are the top-performing states.

Customer Behavior: COD (44%) and UPI (21%) dominate payment preferences, showing reliance on cash and digital wallets.

Category Trends: Clothing drives the majority of sales (63%), followed by Electronics (21%) and Furniture (17%).

Sub‑Category Profitability: Printers and Bookcases generate notable profits, while Sarees and Accessories lead in quantity.

Seasonal Profit Trends: Monthly profit fluctuates, with peaks in certain months, highlighting seasonal buying patterns.

## ✅ Conclusion
The E‑Commerce Sales Dashboard reveals that while overall sales are strong, profitability varies across categories and months. Clothing dominates sales volume, but electronics and furniture contribute significantly to profit. Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh are key markets, and COD remains the most common payment mode. These insights help businesses optimize product focus, regional strategies, and payment options to drive sustainable growth.
